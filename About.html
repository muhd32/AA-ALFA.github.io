ABOUT A A ALFA

Our core business is the wholesale and retail

A Leader in the Nigeria Oil and Gas Sector.

with its headquarter in Kano, Nigeria. The Company was fully incorporated as A. A ALFA NIGERIA LTD in Nigeria in the year 2008.
